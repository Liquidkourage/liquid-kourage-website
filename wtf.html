What is W.T.F.?

World Trivia Federation (W.T.F.) is our exclusive online trivia league that brings together trivia enthusiasts from around the globe. New quizzes drop 2–3 times per week, giving players time to test their knowledge, strategize with buffs and nerfs, and go head-to-head with opponents worldwide.

Whether you're here for the clever initialisms, the unexpected antics, or the bragging rights, W.T.F. delivers a trivia experience like no other.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for most events. For weddings and large corporate events, 2-3 months in advance is ideal to ensure availability.

Do you require a deposit?

Deposits depend on type of event. The full payment is always due no later than 15 minutes post event.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Please contact us as soon as possible to discuss any changes or cancellations. We will do our best to accommodate your request. Typically if done with 2 weeks notice, we will do a full or partial refund, or we will try to reschedule the event.

Do you travel outside your local area?

Yes! We travel throughout the region. Travel fees may apply for events outside our standard service area, which we'll discuss during the booking process.

Can you accommodate special requests?

Absolutely! We love creating custom experiences. Just let us know your special requests in the booking form or during our consultation.

What if I have a last-minute event?

We do our best to accommodate last-minute requests based on availability. Contact us directly for urgent bookings.

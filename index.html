About Liquid Kourage Entertainment

We are an entertainment company that provides trivia, karaoke and DJing for any and all needs! Family owned and local we strive to provide the best quality at affordable prices to make your event or venue as successful as possible. Liquid Kourage was started back in 2003 as a karaoke company by founding member Jay Borsom and a close friend. It all started as an idea of how to make karaoke nights better. Since, Jay has expanded into trivia as well as DJing private parties and weddings. Each type of service we provide has begun with the thought of how we could do this better. As the pandemic came we brought our company online to Twitch and for private events through Zoom or Youtube.

Liquid Kourage has won multiple "Best of the Burbs" awards in the years that the publication was circulating. Since we have connected with international and national communities of trivia hosts and companies through our online experiences. In the last 15+ years we have hosted thousands of events, done hundreds of weddings and frequently hosted private parties in all our areas of entertainment.

Today, Jay and co-owner/wife "Quincy" split duties managing Liquid Kourage Entertainment, LLC and co-hosting weddings and other private events. We have very talented people that also go out to host trivia and karaoke shows in our local area. So no matter what you are guaranteed to get the best quality we can provide! Please consider bringing us in as a weekly, bi-weekly or monthly entertainment to your venue or as an all encompassing entertainment company for your next wedding or gathering.

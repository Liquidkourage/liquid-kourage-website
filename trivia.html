Trivia That Brings People Together

At Liquid Kourage, we turn knowledge into connection with engaging, high-energy trivia experiences for every audience. We host weekly trivia nights at local venues, bringing communities together through fun, friendly competition.

Planning something special? Our private and corporate trivia events are fully customizable, perfect for team building, celebrations, or just a great time with friends and colleagues.

Join our online trivia league, the World Trivia Federation to compete with players around the globe, or play along with us on Twitch for fun online interactivity—wherever you are, the game goes on.

Looking for something different? Try our signature event games like Megasheep, Lingo, and many more—each one customizable to your group and guaranteed to get people talking, laughing, and playing together.

Whether in-person or online, Liquid Kourage delivers trivia that's smart, social, and seriously fun.
